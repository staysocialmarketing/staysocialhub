# "George" AI Agent — Premiere Mortgage Centre
*Questionnaire for Corey's father*
*Purpose: Gather context to build a dedicated AI Chief of Staff for running Premiere*

---

Hi [Dad],

We're building you a personal AI agent — think of it as your own #2 that knows the business, keeps you organized, and handles the things that slow you down. To build it right, I need to understand how you work.

This should take about 15–20 minutes. Answer as much or as little as you'd like — we can always fill in gaps later.

---

## About You & the Business

1. How would you describe your role at Premiere day-to-day? What does a typical week look like?

2. What are the 2–3 things that take up most of your time right now?

3. What are the 2–3 things you wish you had more time for?

4. How many agents are currently active at Premiere? How are they organized (teams, regions, levels)?

---

## Communication & Style

5. How do you prefer to communicate — email, phone, text, in-person? Any tools you use daily (Teams, Slack, GHL, etc.)?

6. How would you describe your communication style? (e.g., direct and brief, relationship-focused, formal, casual)

7. Who are your key contacts you communicate with most — internally and externally? (Names and roles — e.g., Sarah in Operations, compliance partners, lenders, etc.)

---

## Operations & Priorities

8. What are the biggest operational challenges at Premiere right now? (e.g., agent onboarding, compliance, communication, lead flow, reporting)

9. Are there recurring tasks or decisions that happen every week or month that you'd like to delegate or automate?

10. What information do you wish you had at your fingertips that currently takes too long to find?

---

## What You Want From an AI Agent

11. If your AI agent could do one thing that would make the biggest difference in your week, what would it be?

12. What would you NOT want your AI agent to handle — things that should always stay with you?

13. How comfortable are you with AI taking action on your behalf (e.g., drafting emails for your review, summarizing reports, organizing information)? Any concerns?

---

## Premiere's Future

14. What are Premiere's top 2–3 priorities over the next 12 months?

15. Is there anything about the Premiere culture or how you do business that the AI should always reflect? (Values, tone, how you treat agents and clients)

---

*That's it. Thank you — this becomes the foundation for George.*
*— Corey & the Stay Social team*
