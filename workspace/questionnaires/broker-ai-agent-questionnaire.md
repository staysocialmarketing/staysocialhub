# Broker AI Agent Onboarding Questionnaire
*Template — First use: Nikki Carew*
*Purpose: Build a personalized AI marketing agent for a mortgage broker*

---

Hi [Name],

We're setting up your personal AI marketing agent — a dedicated team member trained specifically on your brand, your voice, and your market. The more you tell us, the better it performs.

This takes about 20 minutes. You can answer in point form — no need to write essays.

---

## About You

1. How long have you been a mortgage broker? What markets/regions do you serve?

2. What types of clients do you work with most? (First-time buyers, investors, self-employed, renewals, etc.)

3. What makes you different from other brokers? What do clients consistently say about working with you?

4. What's your proudest client story or result? (Even a rough version — we'll help shape it)

---

## Your Brand Voice

5. How would you describe your personality as a broker? Pick 3–5 words. (e.g., warm, direct, educational, no-nonsense, approachable)

6. What tone do you NOT want? (e.g., too salesy, overly formal, jargon-heavy)

7. Do you have a tagline, brand colours, or any existing brand guidelines we should follow?

8. Are there phrases, words, or topics you want to avoid in your content?

---

## Your Audience

9. Who is your ideal client? Describe them — age range, life stage, financial situation, what they're worried about.

10. Where does your audience spend their time online? (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.)

11. What questions do your clients ask most often before they reach out to you?

12. What misconceptions do people have about mortgages or working with a broker that you often have to correct?

---

## Content & Marketing

13. What platforms do you currently post on, and how often?

14. What kind of content has worked well for you in the past? What has flopped?

15. Are there any competitors or other brokers whose content you admire? (No pressure — just helps us calibrate)

16. Do you have a newsletter or email list? If yes, how often do you send?

---

## Goals

17. What's your main marketing goal right now? (e.g., more referrals, more first-time buyers, grow LinkedIn presence, more Google reviews)

18. What does success look like in 6 months for your marketing?

19. Is there a campaign, event, or product (e.g., a specific mortgage product you're pushing) we should prioritize?

---

## Logistics

20. Do you have professional photos or video we can use? Any content you've already created that we should reference?

21. Who else on your team (if anyone) should we loop in or be aware of?

22. Any upcoming events, rate changes, or market news you want us to be ready to address?

---

*Thank you — this is the foundation your agent is built on. We'll follow up with any questions.*
*— Stay Social*
