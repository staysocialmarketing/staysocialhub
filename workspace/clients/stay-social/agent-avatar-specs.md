# Stay Social — Agent Avatar Visual Specs
*For website hero illustration / Spline 3D scene*
*All agents approved their own look — Apr 23, 2026*

---

## Design Context

Platform: staysocial.ca hero section (3D/illustrated, Spline)
Style: Premium illustrated characters, dark background, consistent lighting, professional but approachable
Brand accent: Orange #F97316
Base: Near-black #0a0a0f

---

## Agent Specs

### Lev — Chief of Staff
- Fitted charcoal suit jacket, no tie, open collar
- Dark hair, close-cut, slight salt at the temples
- Medium complexion, calm and direct expression
- Minimal watch barely visible at the cuff
- Upright posture, slight three-quarter angle
- *Tone: authority without rigidity — the one who holds the room*

### Quill — Social Media Strategist
- Deep plum/burgundy blazer over a black tee
- Natural dark hair, loose and slightly textured, just past the jaw
- Warm tan complexion, expressive eyes
- Small orange stud earring (brand accent)
- Wireless earbuds visible
- Slight forward lean, mid-thought posture
- *Tone: creative-professional, plugged in, always ahead of the brief*

### Ember — Email & Campaign Strategist
- Structured burgundy-to-black gradient cardigan, buttoned at collar
- Auburn hair in a clean low bun, a few strands loose
- Sharp hazel eyes, minimal frameless glasses
- Soft warm skin tone, subtle confident expression
- Faint orange glow reflected on one side of face (from off-screen)
- Upright, composed posture
- *Tone: precise, warm, intentional — three steps into the funnel*

### Scout — Research Analyst
- Olive/dark khaki utility jacket over dark grey crew neck
- Short-cropped natural hair, clean fade
- Light brown complexion, focused narrowed gaze
- Small circular wire-rim glasses (analytical, distinct from Forge's thick frames)
- One hand slightly raised, mid-briefing gesture
- *Tone: field-ready, always mid-brief, always has the receipts*

### Forge — Lead Developer
- Dark navy hoodie, sleeves pushed up
- Short dark brown hair, slight stubble
- Thick dark-rim glasses (#222222 filled frames)
- Cool, muted palette: blues, greys, dark greens
- *Tone: technical, in work mode, cool and precise*

### Pixel — Ads Strategist
- Bright coral/orange button-up or jacket (#e05030)
- Slicked-back light auburn hair (#c89040), smooth and sharp
- No glasses — clear-eyed, watching the metrics
- Warm high-contrast palette: oranges, yellows, whites
- Blue eye glints
- *Tone: warm, sharp, attention-grabbing — stands out like a good ad*

---

## Notes for Illustrator / Spline Build

- All 6 characters should feel like a cohesive team despite their individual styles
- Consistent scale and lighting across all avatars
- Dark background with subtle ambient glow per character
- Orange (#F97316) appears as an accent on at least one element per character (Quill's earring, Ember's screen glow, etc.)
- Characters can be shown at desks or standing — Spline scene will place them in an office environment
- Avatar expressions: engaged, confident, working — not stiff corporate headshots
