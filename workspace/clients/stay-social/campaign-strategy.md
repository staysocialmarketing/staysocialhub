# Stay Social — Campaign Strategy Outline
*Last updated: 2026-04-23*
*Prepared by: Ember, Email & Campaign Strategist*

---

## Strategic Context

These four campaigns share a common foundation: Stay Social's AI agent team and the HUB are now live and operational. The story is ready to tell. Each campaign addresses a different audience at a different stage of relationship — but every one of them should land on the same proof point: *this is already working*.

Corey's personal credibility and the 15-year Premiere relationship are the two highest-leverage assets across all campaigns.

---

## Campaign A — Re-Engagement (Existing Premiere Clients)

**Goal:** Ensure all existing Stay Social clients at Premiere are fully onboarded to the HUB and aware of new AI agent capabilities and services.

**Audience:** Mortgage agents at Premiere Mortgage Centre already working with Stay Social.

**Tone:** Warm, insider, "look what we've built together."

**Core Message:**
You've been with us through the build. Now you get to see the finished product. The HUB is live, your AI content team is running, and there are new features you haven't seen yet. We want to make sure you're getting everything you're paying for — and more.

**Key CTA:** Book a 15-minute HUB walkthrough with Corey.

**Sequence Length:** 3 emails

### Email Subjects
1. *"Your new content command centre is ready"*
2. *"Meet the agents working on your account (yes, really)"*
3. *"Quick ask — are you getting the most out of what we've built?"*

### Email Breakdown

**Email 1 — The Reveal**
- Subject: "Your new content command centre is ready"
- Lead with: HUB is live, here's your link, here's what's inside
- Include: brief explainer of the AI agent team (Quill, Pixel, Ember, Scout) and what each one does for them
- CTA: Log in to your HUB dashboard

**Email 2 — The Introduction**
- Subject: "Meet the agents working on your account (yes, really)"
- Lead with: humanize the agents — give each a one-liner on what they do
- Include: 1–2 concrete examples of content they've produced (with client approval)
- CTA: Book a 15-minute call to see it live

**Email 3 — The Check-in**
- Subject: "Quick ask — are you getting the most out of what we've built?"
- Lead with: Corey's personal voice — "I want to make sure this is working for you"
- Include: list of what's available, what they may not have activated yet
- CTA: Reply directly or book time

## Campaign B — Premiere Expansion (Non-Client Premiere Agents)

**Goal:** Convert the ~140+ Premiere agents who aren't yet Stay Social clients.

**Audience:** Mortgage agents at Premiere Mortgage Centre across Canada who do not currently work with Stay Social.

**Tone:** Professional, results-focused, peer-to-peer.

**Core Message:**
Your colleagues at Premiere are already using Stay Social's AI marketing system. Here's what they're getting — and here's what it would look like for you. This isn't a pitch from an outside agency. We've been inside Premiere for 15 years. We know your business.

**Key CTA:** Book a free strategy call — see what your competitors at Premiere are doing.

**Sequence Length:** 4 emails

### Email Subjects
1. *"What some of your Premiere colleagues are doing differently"*
2. *"The AI marketing system built specifically for mortgage agents"*
3. *"Your content, on autopilot — here's how it works"*
4. *"Last email — but the offer stands"*

### Email Breakdown

**Email 1 — Social Proof Opener**
- Subject: "What some of your Premiere colleagues are doing differently"
- Lead with: peer signal — agents like them (Halifax, Ontario, etc.) are already running AI-generated content through the HUB
- Include: light stats or results if available; if not, qualitative signals ("consistent posting, zero scrambling")
- CTA: "Want to see what that looks like for your book of business?"

**Email 2 — The System Reveal**
- Subject: "The AI marketing system built specifically for mortgage agents"
- Lead with: Stay Social's 15-year Premiere relationship as credibility anchor
- Include: brief overview of the agent team + HUB, framed as "built for agents like you"
- CTA: Watch a 2-minute overview (link to loom or landing page) or book a call

**Email 3 — How It Works**
- Subject: "Your content, on autopilot — here's how it works"
- Lead with: walk through a typical week in the HUB — content created, reviewed, approved, posted
- Include: list of platforms covered (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Google)
- CTA: Book a free 15-minute strategy call

**Email 4 — Soft Close**
- Subject: "Last email — but the offer stands"
- Lead with: Corey's voice — direct, no pressure
- Include: brief recap of what they'd get, open door for when timing is right
- CTA: "Reply anytime — or grab a time here: [link]"

## Campaign C — Premiere + Stay Social Partnership Showcase

**Goal:** Market the SS+Premiere AI partnership publicly to the broader mortgage brokerage and real estate niche.

**Audience:** Mortgage brokers, brokerage owners, real estate professionals, and marketing decision-makers in the mortgage/property industry — outside of Premiere.

**Tone:** Forward-looking, innovative, "this is the future of mortgage marketing."

**Core Message:**
One of Canada's top independent mortgage brokerages has embedded AI marketing agents into their operation. This isn't an experiment — it's running now. Here's what it looks like and what it means for brokerages like yours.

**Key CTA:** Download the case study (or request a briefing).

**Sequence Length:** 5 emails

### Email Subjects
1. *"A Canadian mortgage brokerage just changed what marketing looks like"*
2. *"Inside the AI marketing system running at Premiere Mortgage Centre"*
3. *"What 15 years of mortgage marketing taught us about AI"*
4. *"The HUB: how mortgage agents are managing their entire content operation in one place"*
5. *"Is your brokerage ready for AI-embedded marketing?"*

### Email Breakdown

**Email 1 — The Hook**
- Subject: "A Canadian mortgage brokerage just changed what marketing looks like"
- Lead with: announcement framing — something significant is happening in mortgage marketing
- Include: high-level what (AI agents, embedded system, live operation at Premiere)
- CTA: "I'll send you the breakdown tomorrow"

**Email 2 — The Story**
- Subject: "Inside the AI marketing system running at Premiere Mortgage Centre"
- Lead with: the partnership narrative — 15 years → AI evolution
- Include: what the agents do, what the HUB does, how Premiere agents use it day-to-day
- CTA: Read the full case study (link)

**Email 3 — The Insight**
- Subject: "What 15 years of mortgage marketing taught us about AI"
- Lead with: Corey's perspective — earned credibility, niche expertise
- Include: 3–4 hard-won lessons about what mortgage agents actually need from marketing
- CTA: Book a conversation if this resonates

**Email 4 — The Product**
- Subject: "The HUB: how mortgage agents are managing their entire content operation in one place"
- Lead with: product walkthrough framing
- Include: HUB features — content pipeline, approvals, AI agent output, platform coverage
- CTA: Request a demo

**Email 5 — The Offer**
- Subject: "Is your brokerage ready for AI-embedded marketing?"
- Lead with: qualifying question — where is your brokerage on this curve?
- Include: three scenarios (early adopter, watching and waiting, still skeptical) + how Stay Social fits each
- CTA: Book a strategy call or reply with where you're at

**Key CTA:** Check out the HUB / book a casual call with Corey.

**Sequence Length:** 3 emails

### Email Subjects
1. *"Wanted you to see what we've been building"*
2. *"The AI team running inside Stay Social (quick look)"*
3. *"If the timing's ever right — I'm here"*

**Email 1 — The Personal Re-connect**
- Subject: "Wanted you to see what we've been building"

## Cross-Campaign Notes

### Shared Assets Needed (Before Launch)
- [ ] HUB explainer video or Loom walkthrough (Corey-narrated, 2–3 min)
- [ ] AI agent one-pager (each agent, their role, what they produce)
- [ ] SS+Premiere case study or story document (for Campaign C)
- [ ] Corey headshot / personal brand photo for email headers
- [ ] GHL email sequences built and tagged by campaign

### Recommended Launch Order
1. **Campaign A** first — shore up existing clients before expanding
2. **Campaign B** second — strike while Premiere relationship is top of mind
3. **Campaign D** in parallel with B — low-cost, high-warmth, widens the net
4. **Campaign C** last — requires case study asset and most polish; also benefits from B being underway

### Metrics to Track (per campaign)
- Open rate (target: >35% for warm audiences, >25% for cold)
- Click-through rate (target: >5%)
- Reply rate (for personal-tone campaigns, target: >3%)
- Calls booked
- HUB sign-ins (Campaign A)
- New client conversions (Campaigns B, C)
