The Johnson is a newly developed apartment building managed by Cornwall Capital. It is positioned as a modern, well-amenitized rental option in Cornwall's relatively thin rental market. The property appears to be a newer build targeting renters who want quality finishes and included conveniences without the premium price point of larger Ontario cities.

## Target Audience

### Primary: Working Professionals / Dual-Income Households
- Renters aged 25–45 who want modern finishes and conveniences (in-unit laundry, parking) without the high cost of Ottawa or Toronto
- Value transparency in pricing — cost is the #1 FAQ on the site
- Budget-conscious but not low-income; $2,280/month is above Cornwall's market average

### Secondary: Relocators & Newcomers to Cornwall
- People moving to Cornwall for work, lower cost of living, or proximity to Ottawa (~100km west)
- Searching for quality rental options in an unfamiliar market
- Likely using Google to research "best apartments Cornwall Ontario"

### Secondary: Downsizers
- Couples or individuals moving from larger homes who want low-maintenance living with included amenities and outdoor space

### Market Context
- Cornwall's average 2-bedroom rent is $895–$1,875/month (Zumper, Dec 2025)
- The Johnson at $2,280/month is **above market average** — positioning it as a premium product in this market
- Only ~9 rental listings available per month in Cornwall — low inventory means demand is real, but the price premium requires justification
- 89% of Cornwall listings are pet-friendly — The Johnson's ambiguous pet policy is a potential weakness

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Current Positioning
The existing website copy is **functional and fact-driven** — it answers practical questions clearly but lacks emotional resonance or lifestyle aspiration. Messaging is transparent and trustworthy but not particularly compelling.

### Tone Characteristics
- Professional, clear, no-nonsense
- Accessibility-focused (practical benefit language: "parking included," "we pay water")
- Welcoming but not warm — informational rather than aspirational
- No tagline or brand manifesto visible

### Implied Brand Promise
"Everything you need, included — just move in."

### Recommended Brand Voice Direction (for Ads)
Lean into **modern simplicity meets value clarity**. The Johnson should feel like the obvious smart choice for Cornwall renters who want quality without the chaos of a big-city market. Aspirational but grounded — not luxury, not budget, but "done right."

## Platforms

### Google Ads (Primary Focus)

**Why Google Ads:**
Cornwall has limited inventory (~9 listings/month) and a growing base of relocators and newcomers actively searching. High-intent search terms ("Cornwall Ontario apartments for rent," "2 bedroom Cornwall ON") are the primary acquisition channel. The Johnson needs to be the first result renters see.

**Ad Format Priorities:**
1. **Search Ads** — Capture active rental intent. High-converting because users are already searching.
2. **Performance Max** — Combine search + display + YouTube for broader reach to relocators who haven't decided on Cornwall yet.
3. **Display Remarketing** — Retarget site visitors who didn't convert (key given the considered nature of rental decisions).

**Geographic Targeting:**
- Primary: Cornwall, ON
- Secondary: Ottawa (people searching for more affordable alternatives), Kingston, Montreal (bilingual market — Cornwall has a francophone community)

**Device:** Mobile-first — most rental searches happen on phone

**Conversion Goal:** Phone call to 613-209-2194 (current CTA) or a contact form submission (if one is added)

## Notes on Competitive Positioning

The Johnson is priced **20–40% above the Cornwall market average** for 2-bedroom units. This is not a weakness if positioned correctly — it signals quality and included value in a market where many buildings are older stock with shared laundry and no parking. The key ad strategy is to **justify the premium through included value**, not compete on price.

Competitors are likely older buildings with:
- Shared or coin laundry
- Paid parking or no parking
- Older kitchens
- No private outdoor space

The Johnson's bundle (in-unit laundry + parking + water + outdoor space) may effectively reduce the true cost gap significantly for renters who currently pay separately for these.
