Let me tell you what I'm actually seeing this summer in Winnipeg.

$300,000 to $380,000: well-maintained homes in Transcona, St. Vital, and West Kildonan are moving in a matter of days, usually with multiple offers. Buyers who have their financing ready are winning. Buyers who are still deciding are watching it happen.

$380,000 to $450,000: there's more room to breathe. Homes in River Heights, Wolseley, and parts of St. Boniface are drawing serious buyers who have done their homework. There's still competition, but there's also room for a real conversation.

Above $450,000: things are quieter. Sellers are pricing carefully, and buyers are taking their time. That's a healthy part of the market.

What I keep telling people this summer: if you've been thinking about it, now is a genuinely good time to at least start the conversation. Get pre-approved, know your number, then decide.
