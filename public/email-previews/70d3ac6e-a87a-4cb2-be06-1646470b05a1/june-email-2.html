You may have met the Wheeler Street Muppets on our social media. Today, they're starring in what might be Halifax's most educational mortgage email ever.

Chapter One: The Pre-Approval.

Our Muppet first-timer, let's call him Arlo, had been renting in the South End for four years. He knew vaguely that he should "do something about a mortgage" but kept putting it off. One Saturday morning, he finally called us. He expected a lecture. He got a conversation. We pulled his credit, reviewed his income, and had a pre-approval number back to him within 48 hours. Arlo had no idea it could move that fast.

Chapter Two: The Offer.

Two months into looking, Arlo found a place in Dartmouth. Good bones, manageable commute, a small backyard he immediately started planning a garden for. He was nervous about the offer. We walked him through every number — payment, closing costs, what his first year would actually look like. The offer went in.

Chapter Three: The Keys.

Arlo is now a homeowner. He asked me afterward if the process was always that smooth. Honestly? Not always. But when you're prepared, and you know what to expect at each step, it goes better than you think.

If you're in Arlo's shoes right now, wondering if it could actually happen for you — let's find out.
