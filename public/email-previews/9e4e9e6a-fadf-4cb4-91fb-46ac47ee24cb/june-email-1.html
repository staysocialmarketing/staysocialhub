Last month I was reviewing a client file and noticed something she didn't know existed.

She'd been on a variable rate product for two years. Rates had moved. Her payment had adjusted. But the rate she was paying was 35 basis points higher than what I could get her today through a different lender — same term, same structure, better rate. Not because her situation had changed. Because the market moved and nobody went looking.

We restructured it. Her payment dropped. She texted me a week later to say she was redirecting the savings into her daughter's RESP.

That's the kind of thing I look for every month. Pain. Opportunities. Protection. I run that check on every active file — not to generate a transaction, but to make sure nothing has shifted in the market or changed in a client's situation that means they should be doing something differently.

Most mortgage agents are reactive. You come to them when you need something. Blue Rhino is built to be proactive. That means I'm reviewing files when you're not thinking about your mortgage, and sometimes finding things that matter.

If you've been with your lender for more than a year and nobody has checked whether you're still in the right position, that's the conversation to have.
