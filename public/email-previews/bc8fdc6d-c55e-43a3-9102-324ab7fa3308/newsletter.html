Tip of the Issue

Don't auto-renew without checking your options first.

> Your lender's renewal offer is rarely their best rate - it's their opening number. If you simply sign and return, you're often leaving money on the table without realizing it. <

The good news: renewal time is also the easiest moment to shop around. No new appraisal, no stress test if you switch lenders at the same balance. A quick conversation before you sign can make a real difference.

 

Question I Keep Hearing

> "I'm self-employed - is it even possible for me to qualify?" <

> Absolutely - and you have more options than you think. Lenders have come a long way on self-employed files. The key is knowing how to present your income correctly.
